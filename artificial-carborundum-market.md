<p><p>The Artificial Carborundum market primarily includes two types: Green Silicon Carbide and Black Silicon Carbide. Green Silicon Carbide is produced through a high-temperature process, resulting in a purer form ideal for high-performance applications, particularly in the automotive and aerospace sectors. Black Silicon Carbide, on the other hand, is derived from the reduction of silica and is used mainly for abrasives and cutting tools due to its hardness and thermal conductivity. Both types cater to diverse industries, influencing demand dynamics globally.</p></p>
