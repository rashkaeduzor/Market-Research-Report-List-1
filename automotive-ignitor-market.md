<p><strong>The Automotive Ignitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-controlled Ignition System</li><li>Breakless Ignition System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Contact-controlled Ignition System** uses mechanical contact points to manage the timing and distribution of electrical current to the spark plugs, ensuring combustion in the engine. It is a traditional system but prone to wear and maintenance. The **Breakerless Ignition System** replaces mechanical contacts with electronic components, offering improved reliability, precision, and reduced maintenance. It uses sensors and control modules to manage spark timing, enhancing engine performance and fuel efficiency. Both systems are integral to automotive ignition but differ in technology and operational efficiency.</p></p>
