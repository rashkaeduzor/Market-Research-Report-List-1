<p><ul><li>Hot-dip Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh</li><li>Cold Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized welded wire mesh market consists of two primary types: hot-dip galvanized welded wire mesh and cold galvanized welded wire mesh. Hot-dip galvanized welded wire mesh undergoes a process where the wire is dipped in molten zinc, providing superior corrosion resistance and durability, suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, cold galvanized welded wire mesh uses a zinc coating applied through electroplating, offering basic protection against rust and is often used in lighter applications where corrosion risk is lower.</p></p>
