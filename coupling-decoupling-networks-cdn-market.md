<p><p>Coupling Decoupling Networks (CDNs) are essential in the EMC radiation and immunity testing markets, as they facilitate the accurate measurement of electromagnetic emissions and susceptibility. In EMC radiation tests, CDNs couple signals to the device under test while isolating external noise, ensuring precise assessments of emissions. For EMC immunity tests, CDNs decouple unwanted signals, protecting the test equipment from interference. These functions are crucial for compliance with international standards, enabling manufacturers to ensure product reliability and safety in electromagnetic environments.</p></p>
