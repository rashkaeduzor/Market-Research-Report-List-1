<p><p>The force sensors market features various types catering to different applications. Piezoresistive force sensors detect changes in resistance under pressure, while ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure force. Capacitive sensors monitor changes in capacitance, and piezoelectric sensors generate voltage when force is applied. Strain gauges measure deformation to quantify force, optical sensors utilize light interference for precise measurements, and magnetic sensors rely on magnetic fields. Each type offers unique benefits, making them suitable for specific industrial, automotive, and consumer applications.</p></p>
