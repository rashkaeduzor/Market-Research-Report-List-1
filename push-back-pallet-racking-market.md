<p><p>Push Back Pallet Racking is an innovative storage system designed to increase warehouse efficiency by allowing pallets to be stored and retrieved in a more compact manner. This system utilizes a series of nested carts on inclined rails, enabling pallets to be pushed back from the front when new pallets are added, and retrieved from the front when needed. It optimizes space utilization while enhancing accessibility to stored goods, making it ideal for operations with a high volume of inventory turnover.</p><p>Market growth for Push Back Pallet Racking is being driven by the increasing demand for efficient warehouse management solutions, particularly in sectors such as retail, e-commerce, and manufacturing.
