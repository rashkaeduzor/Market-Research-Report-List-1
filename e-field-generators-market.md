<p><p>The E-Field Generators market comprises two primary types: Horizontal Electric Field Generators and Vertical Electric Field Generators. Horizontal Electric Field Generators are designed to produce electric fields parallel to the ground, commonly used in applications like electromagnetic compatibility testing. In contrast, Vertical Electric Field Generators create fields directed upward or downward, often utilized in research and development scenarios. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, by ensuring device compliance and performance testing.</p></p>
