<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) lamp market primarily comprises two key types: UV mercury lamps and UV LED lamps. UV mercury lamps utilize mercury vapor to produce ultraviolet light, offering high intensity and efficiency for industrial applications, such as disinfection and curing processes. Conversely, UV LED lamps use semiconductor technology, providing lower energy consumption, longer lifespan, and reduced heat generation, making them ideal for portable and consumer applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in various sectors, including healthcare, printing, and water treatment.</p></p>
