<p><ul><li>Continuity Measurement</li><li>Voltage Measurement</li><li>Resistance Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog megohmmeter market is categorized into three primary types: continuity measurement, voltage measurement, and resistance measurement. Continuity measurement ensures that electrical circuits are complete and functional, detecting breaks or faults. Voltage measurement assesses the voltage levels across components to ensure safe operation. Resistance measurement evaluates the resistance of insulation materials to gauge their effectiveness in preventing current leakage. Together, these measurements are crucial for ensuring electrical safety and system reliability across various applications.</p></p>
