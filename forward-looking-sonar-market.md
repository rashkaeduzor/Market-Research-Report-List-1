<p><p>Forward Looking Sonar (FLS) markets can be categorized into two types: 3D and 2D. The 3D type provides a volumetric view of underwater environments, allowing for enhanced perception of underwater obstacles and terrain, thus offering detailed spatial awareness. In contrast, the 2D type presents a flat, top-down view, primarily focusing on detecting objects directly ahead. Both types serve diverse applications in naval, commercial, and research fields, with the choice depending on specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
