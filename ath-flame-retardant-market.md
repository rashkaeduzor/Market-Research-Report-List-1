<p><p>The ATH flame retardant market comprises two main types: Ground/Milled ATH and Precipitated ATH. Ground/Milled ATH is derived from grinding bauxite ore into fine particles, providing excellent thermal stability and smoke suppression, making it suitable for various applications, including plastics and textiles. On the other hand, Precipitated ATH is produced through precipitation processes, offering high purity and finer particle size, enhancing its performance in coatings and rubber industries. Both types contribute significantly to improving fire safety in materials.</p></p>
