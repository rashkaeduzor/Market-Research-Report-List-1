<p><ul><li>Dry-SP</li><li>Wet-SP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sandpaper market is categorized into two main types: dry sandpaper (Dry-SP) and wet sandpaper (Wet-SP). Dry-SP is designed for use without water, making it ideal for sanding wood, metal, and plastic. It produces finer finishes on dry surfaces. In contrast, Wet-SP is used with water or other lubricants, reducing dust and extraneous particles, while providing smoother finishes, especially on softer materials. Both types cater to different applications and user preferences in woodworking and finishing tasks.</p></p>
