<p><strong>The Industrial Potato Graders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Type</li><li>Grading Web Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial potato graders market comprises primarily two types: roller type and grading web type. Roller type graders utilize rotating rollers to separate potatoes based on size as they move along a conveyor, ensuring efficient sorting. In contrast, grading web type graders use a flexible mesh conveyor with strategically placed apertures, allowing potatoes to fall through based on their size. Both systems enhance sorting accuracy, streamline processing operations, and contribute to improved quality control in potato handling and packaging.</p></p>
