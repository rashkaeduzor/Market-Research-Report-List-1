<p><ul><li>Flame Spraying</li><li>Arc Spraying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal sprayed aluminum coatings market comprises various application methods, primarily flame spraying and arc spraying. Flame spraying utilizes a combustion process to melt aluminum wire, which is then propelled onto a substrate, creating a robust coating. In contrast, arc spraying employs an electric arc to melt two aluminum wires, which are atomized and sprayed onto the surface, ensuring a uniform and dense layer. Both techniques enhance corrosion resistance and longevity of metal surfaces, making them essential in various industrial applications.</p></p>
