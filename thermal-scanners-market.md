<p><p>The thermal scanners market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: LWIR (Long-Wave Infrared), MWIR (Mid-Wave Infrared), and SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared). LWIR scanners are widely used for their ability to detect heat from objects in varying environmental conditions, making them popular in surveillance and building inspections. MWIR scanners are effective for higher temperature detection, often used in military and industrial applications. SWIR scanners are ideal for material inspection and imaging in low-light conditions, providing high-resolution images.</p></p>
