<p><p>Biodegradable plastic is a type of plastic designed to decompose naturally through the action of microorganisms, such as bacteria and fungi, under specific environmental conditions. These plastics are often made from renewable resources like corn starch, sugarcane, or cellulose, as well as from petrochemicals with added biodegradable additives.</p><p>The Biodegradable Plastic Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of environmental issues, stringent regulations on single-use plastics, and the rising demand for sustainable packaging solutions.
