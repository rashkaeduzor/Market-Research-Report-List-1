<p><p>Decorative plywood is categorized by thickness into three market types: Below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and Above 18mm. Below 6mm plywood is lightweight, ideal for furniture backing, paneling, and decorative applications. The 6mm-18mm range is versatile, commonly used for cabinetry, wall cladding, and medium-duty furniture due to its balance of strength and flexibility. Above 18mm plywood is thicker and sturdier, suitable for heavy-duty applications like flooring, structural work, and industrial furniture. Each type caters to specific needs based on durability, weight, and intended use.</p></p>
