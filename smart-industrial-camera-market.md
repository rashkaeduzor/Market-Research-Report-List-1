<p><strong>The Smart Industrial Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Industrial Camera market comprises two primary types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture full images of an entire scene at once, making them suitable for stationary applications and capturing 2D images. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, allowing for high-resolution imaging of moving objects, making them ideal for industrial applications like web inspection. Both types enhance automation and quality control in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
